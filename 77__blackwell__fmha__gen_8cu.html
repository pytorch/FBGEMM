Detailed Description

MQA/GQA

The head dimension can be represented as a tuple, where the K/V strides in the first dimension is zero. This has the effect of MQA or GQA. MHA is (head_size:head_stride). MQA is (head_size:head_stride) in Q and (head_size:_0) in K and V. GQA is (grouped_heads,heads_kv):(head_stride,grouped_heads*head_stride) in Q and (grouped_heads,heads_kv):(0,head_stride) in K and V

Example usage: $ ./examples/77_blackell_fmha/77_blackell_fmha_gen_fp8 \ –b=2048 –h=2048 –d=2048 –k=2048
