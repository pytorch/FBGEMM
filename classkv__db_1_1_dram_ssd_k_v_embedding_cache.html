Detailed Description

template<typename weight_type>
class kv_db::DramSsdKVEmbeddingCache< weight_type >

This is the forwarding skeleton: every EmbeddingKVDB method delegates to the wrapped DRAM cache. The SSD (L3) tier and its orchestration (two-phase lookup, async backfill, eviction writeback, flush-to-SSD, and checkpoint reads) are layered on top in a follow-up.
